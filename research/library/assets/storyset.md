# Storyset

## What it is

Free customizable illustrations and **animated** illustrations from Freepik. You can change colors, backgrounds, character poses, and download as SVG, PNG, or animated SVG/Lottie/After Effects.

## License

**Free with attribution** to Freepik. (Premium subscription removes attribution requirement.)

## Used for

Marketing pages, animated empty states, onboarding flows. Especially useful when you want movement (an animated loading state, a hero illustration that subtly moves).

## How to use

1. Go to https://storyset.com
2. Search by keyword or browse by style (Cuate, Pana, Bro, Amico, etc.)
3. Customize colors and background
4. Download (free version requires attribution link in footer)

## Score: 7/10 for Adil

Useful, but the attribution requirement makes unDraw a better default. Use Storyset when you specifically need animation.

## Risks

- **Attribution required** for free use — read terms before using in a commercial product without buying premium
- Aesthetic is more "marketing"-flavored than "dashboard"-flavored
